
Privacy Policy – CareStation App
Last Updated: April 24, 2026  

Introduction
Welcome to CareStation (also known as "Doctors Appointments"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our app.  

By using CareStation, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.  

---

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information
When you register or use the app, we may collect the following:  

Account Information:  
- Full name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Password (encrypted)  
- Profile picture  

Patient Information:  
- Medical history  
- Medical appointments  
- Prescriptions  
- Health records  

Payment Information  

Location Information:  
- GPS services to find nearby doctors and clinics  
- Precise and approximate location data when permissions are enabled  

Usage Data:  
- Search history and preferences  

1.2 Required Permissions
The app requests the following permissions:  
- Camera: to capture profile photos and medical documents  
- Location: to find nearby doctors and clinics  
- Storage: to save photos and documents  
- Internet: to connect to servers and update data  
- Notifications: to send appointment reminders and important alerts  

---

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Service Delivery
- Create and manage your account  
- Book appointments with doctors and clinics  
- Process payments and transactions  
- Provide telemedicine consultations via video  
- Manage pharmacies and medication requests  

2.2 Improvement and Development
- Enhance user experience  
- Develop new features  
- Analyze usage patterns  
- Fix bugs and technical issues  

2.3 Communication
- Send appointment reminders  
- Provide service updates  
- Respond to inquiries and support requests  

2.4 Security and Compliance
- Prevent fraud and illegal activities  
- Protect user rights and the app  
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements  

---

3. Sharing Information

3.1 With Medical Service Providers
We share your information with:  
- Doctors and clinics you book appointments with  
- Pharmacies to process medication requests  
- Medical laboratories when needed  

3.2 With External Service Providers
We use third-party services including:  

Firebase (Google):  
- Authentication (login)  
- Cloud database (Firestore)  
- Cloud storage  
- Push notifications (FCM)  

Google Maps:  
- Maps and location services  
- Nearby place search  
- Telemedicine support  

---

4. Data Security
We take serious measures to protect your information:  
- Encryption: SSL/TLS for data transfer  
- Firebase Authentication: secure login with encrypted passwords  
- Secure Storage: data stored on secure servers  
- Restricted Access: only authorized staff can access data  
- Monitoring: systems monitored for security threats  

---

5. Data Retention
- We retain your personal information as long as your account is active  
- You may request account deletion at any time via app settings  
- After deletion, some data may be retained for legal compliance or dispute resolution  
- Unnecessary data is periodically deleted  

---

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Update
You can access and update your personal information through account settings.  

6.2 Deletion
You can request account and data deletion:  
Settings > Account > Delete Account  

6.3 Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent for data processing and disable permissions from your device settings.  

6.4 Data Portability
You may request a copy of your data in a readable format.  

6.5 Objection
You may object to data processing for certain purposes.

CareStation is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover data collected from a child without parental consent, we will delete it immediately.
